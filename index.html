Trustpilot AI Review Summaries

Most reviewers were let down by their experience overall. Many found it frustrating and tedious, often leading to anger and stress. The AI system was ineffective, providing inadequate feedback and sometimes presenting incorrect answers or getting stuck in loops.

Reviewers describe it as a "punishment simulator" and a "pedagogical disaster," with some stating it taught them "suffering" rather than learning. Some questions were unsolvable even with external help. Mental health issues and even damage to devices reported.

The system punishes struggle and rewards speed, making learning a "soul-crushing, bureaucratic nightmare." An unintuitive interface with clashing neon colors, useless videos, and endless loops of failure — described as "joy-destroying educational software."
